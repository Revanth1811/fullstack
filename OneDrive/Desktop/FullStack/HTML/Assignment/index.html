Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu

Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu (born 9 August 1975) is an Indian actor, producer, media personality,
and philanthropist who works mainly in Telugu cinema. He has appeared in more than 25 films,
and won several accoladesbr including, Eight Nandi Awards, five Film fare Telugu Awards,
four SIIMA Awards, three CineMAA Awards, and one IIFA Utsavam Award.
